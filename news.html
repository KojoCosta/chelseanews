Chelsea still plagued by inconsistency as defensive woes continue in last-gasp Sheffield United draw

Chelsea thought they had put their 2-2 tie at home to 10-man Burnley firmly to bed, but switched off and drew by the same scoreline against Sheffield United, the Premier League’s bottom club. It was a disappointing end to a flat performance, as Noni Madueke thought he had won it with a stunning second-half strike in challenging circumstances.

Cole Palmer Injury update

There were injury fears for Palmer when he was taken off with 20 minutes to go in Yorkshire, particularly after a hefty challenge from Jack Robinson which earned the Blades man a booking. Mauricio Pochettino confirmed post-match that he had picked up a knock in the Manchester United match but his substitution was instead due to being “little bit more tired than normal”. Potential return date: April 15, vs Everton

07 April 2024

Malo Gusto

Malo Gusto

Gusto dropped out of the team on the weekend having recently suffered from illness. Chelsea expect to have him back soon after Pochettino confirmed: “Malo has to rest. He was ill before playing two games, so he has to rest.” Potential return date: April 15, vs Everton
